# Code of Conduct — Chaser Agent

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Chaser Agent community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

Examples of positive behaviour: respectful and constructive communication;
graceful acceptance of feedback; focusing on what is best for the project.

Unacceptable behaviour: harassment, personal attacks, publishing others' private
information, or other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces (issues, discussions, reviews)
and when an individual represents the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report violations to conduct@chaseos.ai. Reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly. Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, from a
warning to a permanent ban.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the Contributor Covenant.
